About Shree Hita Ambrish Ji

Shree Hita Ambrish ji is a charismatic young spiritual leader from Vrindavan, with a refreshing take on devotion to the divine. He is a guide, teacher and friend to all those who seek truth or are looking for self-realization, enlightenment & liberation.

Much respected Ambrish ji is fondly called “Maharaj Ji”; and has been giving spiritual talks in India and abroad, since 2006 on “Bhakti Yoga”. He expresses his love in the lotus feet of Shree Radha and elaborates the splendour, majesty and eminence in his sermons. He elucidates “Seva” as the purest form of love towards the divine power.

The new generation widely accepts his doctrine and his way of moralism. Maharaj Ji explains this journey from self to God in utmost simplicity; and considers introspection as the embarkation on this path. To quote him, “it’s a journey from self to inner self”. The sermons delivered by Shree Hita Ambrish ji are generally about the characteristics of numerous “Bhakts” and is based on the composition namely Shrimad Bhaktmaal written by Shree Nabha Ji Maharaj.

About Hita Ras Dhara

Hita Ras Dhara ! A team of devoted followers of the divine being who have been channelizing most of their energy in spreading the fragrance of the divine Love, Kindness and Compassion of the supreme god. We at Hita Ras Dhara believe that, what we have experienced by the grace of Shree Ji must be shared with the entire world and no human being on this planet should remain deprived from this divinity of the lord.

This is only possible by the mentoring of Shree Hita Ambrish Ji, under whose able guidance we are able to progress on this journey. Not only he has been our constant source of inspiration but also has always held our hand and shown us the right path.

We not only share the teachings of Lord Krishna by means of Videos, Audios and Journals so that it reaches the masses, but also aim to foster the feeling of complete surrender within each and every human being towards the divine lotus feet of Shree Radha.
