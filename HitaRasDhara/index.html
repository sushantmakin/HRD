About Hita Ambrish Ji

Shree Hita Ambrish ji is a charismatic young spiritual leader from Vrindavan, with a refreshing take on devotion to the divine. He is a guide, teacher and friend to all those who seek truth or are looking for self-realization, enlightenment & liberation. Much respected Ambrish ji is fondly called “Maharaj Ji”; and has been giving spiritual talks in India and abroad, since 2006 on “Bhakti Yoga”. He expresses his love in the lotus feet of Shree Radha and elaborates the splendour, majesty and eminence in his sermons. He elucidates “Seva” as the purest form of love towards the divine power.

Madhurashtak
(Gorakhpur)

The Madhurasthakam, composed by Sri Vallabhacharya, is a unique stotra, describing the sweetness of Lord Shree Krishna.


Uddhav Sandesh
(Panchkula)

Shree Krishna requesting Uddhav to go to Vrindavan to pacify Nandbaba, Ma Yashoda, the Gopis his ‘Sakhas’ and all Brajvasis, by reminding them of their pastimes with Shree Krishna.

Radha Kirpa Kataksh
(Vrindavan)

A prayer for the merciful sidelong glance of Shree Radha. This stotra is given by Lord Shiva himself and believed to be from the ‘Urdhavamnaya tantra’.


Gopi Geet
(Shah Auditorium, Delhi)

A divine composition by the Gopis in the earnest longing of just a glimpse of their beloved Shree Krishna. When Shree Krishna disappeared at the time of ‘Rasa’
