About Hita Ambrish Ji

Shree Hita Ambrish ji is a charismatic young spiritual leader from Vrindavan, with a refreshing take on devotion to the divine. He is a guide, teacher and friend to all those who seek truth or are looking for self-realization, enlightenment & liberation. Much respected Ambrish ji is fondly called “Maharaj Ji”; and has been giving spiritual talks in India and abroad, since 2006 on “Bhakti Yoga”. He expresses his love in the lotus feet of Shree Radha and elaborates the splendour, majesty and eminence in his sermons. He elucidates “Seva” as the purest form of love towards the divine power.
