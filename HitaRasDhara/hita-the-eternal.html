Hita The Eternal

What is ‘HITA’ the eternal?

HITA - ‘unconditional love’. A divine eternal energy which enriches each and everyones life.

HITA the eternal - is an initiative by Hita Ambrish ji to connect with the youth to share the joy of celebrating life. To share the this eternal happiness of staying with ‘HITA’ (unconditional love) and channelising this divine energy into our daily lives.

HITA the eternal is ...

INTERACTIVE - This platform enables the youth to interact directly with Hita Ambrish ji via posting queries on his website and receive a fresh perspective on their queries via his responses posted on HITA the eternal. These queries are responded to by Hita Ambrish on a regular basis to remain constantly connected with the youth.

EMPOWERMENT OF THOUGHT- To identify and obtain your own solutions, to become masters of your own life and the embark on the journey from confusion to conclusion. To able to craft oneself with the complications and dilemmas that surround us and tap on the positivity within to strengthen yourself.

REFLECT AND ACCEPT - To take ownership of your life and become better acquainted with your own self. Develop a better understanding of the inner self to accept both the strength and weakness within and truly embrace yourself.

EASE AND GRATITUDE- To bring back ease in the daily life and have an attitude of gratitude. To be able clear the clutter of numerous thoughts and noise of all the chaos and confusion, by drawing yourself together, by becoming centered and channelising your inner energy effectively.

MOMENTUM - To enhance the momentum of emotional and spiritual growth. To connect to that ultimate source of love and joy and celebrate each and everyday of your life.

Hita Ambrish ji has conducted such interactive sessions with youth in Delhi with an overwhelming response. These sessions were simple yet very profound invoking a sense to drill in deeper within to unearth numerous possibilities and reduce the clutter of dilemmas and confusions. This platform enabled the youth participants to share their concerns and questions and encourage a meaningful dialogue.
