About Hita Ambrish

Hita Ambrish Ji is young, charismatic, spiritual teacher. An inspiring mentor who has transformed many lives and introduced them to path filled with love, devotion and selfless service. He speaks from the voice of his experience and touches the deepest chord even with a casual listener and is himself a living testimony to all that he teaches.

Hita The Eternal

What is ‘HITA’ the eternal?

HITA - ‘unconditional love’. A divine eternal energy which enriches each and everyones life.
HITA the eternal - is an initiative by Hita Ambrish ji to connect with the youth to share the joy of celebrating life. To share the this eternal happiness of staying with ‘HITA’ (unconditional love) and channelising this divine energy into our daily lives.

Madhurashtak
(Gorakhpur)

The Madhurasthakam, composed by Sri Vallabhacharya, is a unique stotra, describing the sweetness of Lord Shree Krishna.


Uddhav Sandesh
(Panchkula)

Shree Krishna requesting Uddhav to go to Vrindavan to pacify Nandbaba, Ma Yashoda, the Gopis his ‘Sakhas’ and all Brajvasis, by reminding them of their pastimes with Shree Krishna.

Radha Kirpa Kataksh
(Vrindavan)

A prayer for the merciful sidelong glance of Shree Radha. This stotra is given by Lord Shiva himself and believed to be from the ‘Urdhavamnaya tantra’.


Gopi Geet
(Shah Auditorium, Delhi)

A divine composition by the Gopis in the earnest longing of just a glimpse of their beloved Shree Krishna. When Shree Krishna disappeared at the time of ‘Rasa’
